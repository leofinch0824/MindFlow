# Design System Documentation

## Theme Overview
This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our digital product, ensuring consistency and a unified user experience.

### Color Palette
Our color palette is designed for clarity and brand recognition, anchored by a `light` mode.

*   **Primary Color:** `#2C5E6E` - This is the anchor of our brand identity, used for key interactive elements like buttons and calls to action.
*   **Secondary Color:** `#4A4A4A` - A versatile supporting color, suitable for less prominent UI elements and secondary actions.
*   **Neutral Color:** `#F5F5F3` - Our foundational color, providing a clean and consistent backdrop for content and UI components.

### Typography
A harmonious blend of fonts ensures readability and establishes a distinct typographic voice.

*   **Headlines:** `Newsreader` - Chosen for its strong presence and classic appeal, ideal for conveying importance and hierarchy.
*   **Body Text:** `Manrope` - Selected for its excellent legibility and modern feel, ensuring a comfortable reading experience across all content.
*   **Labels:** `Manrope` - Consistent with body text for clarity and uniformity in interactive labels and smaller text elements.

### Shape and Form
The subtle roundedness of our UI elements contributes to a modern and approachable aesthetic, with a value of `1`.

### Spacing
The layout employs `normal` spacing (a value of `2`), balancing density and readability to create a balanced visual flow.
